TROUBLESHOOTING

If you get "404: NOT_FOUND" error:

- Redeploy the app

- Copy the correct POSTGRES_PRISMA_URL
- Update DATABASE_URL in environment variables
- Redeploy

If you get "NEXTAUTH_SECRET not set":
